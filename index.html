#NoProofNoCatch

Stop Illegal Catching — We Demand Proof Before Any Catch

Catching community animals is ONLY legal in 4 cases under AWBI & ABC Rules: Rabies suspected/confirmed · Severe illness/injury · Repeated unprovoked aggression · As per AWBI & ABC Rules. False complaints hurt animals and misuse the law. No Proof = No Catch.

⚖️ AWBI & ABC Rules🚫 No Proof = No Catch📋 3 Types of Proof Required
Legal Proofs Required:
1. 🎥 Proof of Aggression — clear video, date/time/location
2. 🦠 Proof of Rabies — excessive drooling, sudden aggression, disorientation
3. 🏥 Medical/Injury Proof — bite photos, doctor report, vaccination record

GOCAF Vision

A future where people and animals coexist peacefully is not a dream — it is a responsibility. GOCAF exists to make that future real.

To create a world where every community animal lives with dignity, safety, and freedom — supported by structured, lawful, and compassionate human systems.

GOCAF aims to transform animal welfare from chaotic rescue into structured governance — where compassion is backed by policy, technology, and accountability.

Meet Our Founder

Note from the Founder

Deepthika Palati — Founder & Director, GOCAF

"Every animal's life has value. Every voice that speaks for them matters."

GOCAF was founded with a simple yet powerful belief: community animals deserve freedom, dignity, and protection — not conflict, fear, or neglect. Across cities, towns, and villages, animals live alongside us every day. They are part of our shared ecosystem and our shared responsibility.

Over the years, I have witnessed how lack of awareness, misinformation, and absence of structured systems lead to unnecessary suffering. GOCAF was born to bridge this gap.

Our approach:

  • We stand with the law, not above it.
  • We support communities, not divide them.
  • We protect animals, without exploiting emotion or charity.
  • We collaborate, because animal welfare is never a one-organisation mission.

To every volunteer, partner, feeder, resident, and supporter — thank you for choosing compassion with responsibility.

With purpose and resolve,
Deepthika Palati
Founder & Director, GOCAF
