About the Programme
What is S.A.F.E-ABC?

A humane sterilization and care program designed to control dog population humanely, improve animal health and lifespan, and reduce conflict between animals and communities. We ensure end-to-end care, from registration to recovery and safe release.

Control Population Humanely

Stop the cycle of suffering — managed sterilization replaces culling and unnecessary harm with science-backed compassion.

Improve Health & Lifespan

Sterilized animals live longer, healthier lives — reducing disease, injury risk, and preventable deaths on the streets.

Reduce Community Conflict

Calmer, healthier animals mean fewer territorial issues, fewer rabies fears, and safer, more harmonious neighborhoods.

Our Complete Process
Step-by-Step Animal Care Journey

Every animal entering S.A.F.E-ABC moves through six carefully designed stages — built for safety, ethics, and dignity.

1
Registration

The first step for every community animal entering the program.

  • Pet caretaker / Owner / Animal lover registers the animal
  • Animal profile created in SAVE system
  • Caregiver is added to the record
2
Pre-Monitoring (3 Days)

Pre-surgery observation to ensure the animal is fit and ready.

  • Animal is observed for 3 days
  • Vital checks & wellness assessment
  • Ensures the animal is safe and ready for sterilization
3
Government Coordination

Working hand-in-hand with municipal authorities for full compliance.

  • Coordination with GHMC (Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation)
  • Operations follow approved ABC protocols
  • Following ethical and medical standards
4
Sterilization Procedure

Performed in certified, hygienic facilities by trained professionals.

  • Performed by certified veterinary teams
  • Following ethical and medical standards
5
Post-Operation Care

No animal is released until fully recovered and safe.

  • Recovery monitoring by GOCAF team
  • Food, rest, and medical support
  • Continuous health tracking
6
Safe Release

Returned to its home territory — never relocated, never abandoned.

  • Animal is released back to its original location
  • Caregiver is informed
  • Vital signs & health continue to be tracked
Safety First

Why This Process is Safe

  • No relocation stress
  • No abandonment risk
  • Pre and post health monitoring
  • Government-approved ABC methods
  • Trained veterinary supervision
  • Animal passes through SAVE system
Every step is designed to ensure ZERO HARM and MAXIMUM CARE
