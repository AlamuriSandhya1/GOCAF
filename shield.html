About the Programme
What is Project SHIELD?

Project SHIELD is a community-driven shield system where community animals participate in safe, structured, and supervised integration with the people they share neighborhoods with. We:

Identify and register community dogs

Each animal in the area is recorded and assigned to a local caregiver.

Ensure complete medical care & sterilization

Every dog receives vaccinations, sterilization, and ongoing health support.

🩺
Health monitoring & rabies prevention

Continuous health tracking through the SAVE system protects animals and the community.

Provide safe shelter & basic protection

Designated safe spaces, feeding zones, and weather protection for community dogs.

Train friendly animals to live safely with humans

Behavioral preparation that helps dogs and people coexist peacefully and confidently.

Process
How It Works

Five structured steps that turn community concerns into community pride.

1
Community Registration

The starting point — every animal entering the program is officially recorded.

  • Caregivers register their dogs
  • Dogs assigned to a particular zone
  • Community-led approach with localized accountability
2
Health & Safety Check

A clean bill of health makes for a safer community.

  • Vet checkup & health-based registration
  • Vaccination
  • Rabies prevention
3
Training Program

Friendly behavior, never forced — only positive, gradual preparation.

  • Behavior training & bite prevention
  • Trust building with humans
  • Step-by-step monitoring & preparation
4
Community Integration

Dogs return to their environment — now safer for everyone.

  • Dogs are reintegrated into their zone
  • Community awareness sessions
  • Safe co-existence with residents
5
Active Monitoring

Long-term tracking to keep both animals and people safe.

  • Continuous tracking through SAVE system
  • Behavior tracking
  • Periodic health & safety reviews
The SHIELD Difference

What Makes SHIELD Different?

  • No aggressive training — only friendly, positive methods
  • Humane approach for everyone, no judgement
  • Government coordination & compliance
  • Medical care & sterilization included
  • Lifelong tracking & monitoring through SAVE system

Our Commitment
Discipline & Protection Policy

GOCAF supports and enforces a strict, ethical policy for every animal in the program.

  • No relocation
  • No abandonment
  • No abuse / no harm
  • No unnecessary capture
  • Protect with awareness, knowledge, and education
